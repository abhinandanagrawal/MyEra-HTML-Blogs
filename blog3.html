Know our Partners

1. Atal Incubation Centre- Shiv Nadar University (AIC- SNU):

Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) set up at Shiv Nadar University with the support of Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog (https://aim.gov.in/index.php) , to serve as an active catalyst to assist budding entrepreneurs and innovators in their entrepreneurial journey. It is sector agnostic and welcomes start-ups from diverse fields.



AIC-SNU, as a business incubator, is highly flexible and has a unique combination of business development processes, infrastructure and people designed to nurture new and small businesses by helping them to survive and grow through the difficult and vulnerable early stages of development.

Website: https://aic.snu.edu.in/

Email ID: incubator@snu.edu.in

Facebook,Twitter,LinkedIn


2. Atal Incubation Centre- Birla Institute of Management and Technology (AIC- BIMTECH):

AIC- BIMTECH is a sector agnostic business incubator. It supports all selected startups to become successful enterprises through sector-wise expert mentoring and coaching. It has 31 mentors & 61 partnerships. These 61 partnerships give a benefit of INR 8.5 Cr. (Approx 1.2 Mn USD) to each incubatee. The incubator also has an extensive network of domestic and international investors.


It has 3 programs- Khoj (Ideation to Prototype), Gurukul (First Level Revenue Generation) and Udaan (Acceleration). Startups can apply in the category that best suits them.

Website: https://www.aicbimtech.com/

Email ID: aic.bimtech@bimtech.ac.in

Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn


3. Atal Incubation Centre- Rambhau Mhalgi Prabodhini (AIC-RMP):

AIC - RMP (AIC) Atal Incubation Centre- Rambhau Mhalgi Prabodhini (RMP) Foundation has been set up in alignment with Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), NITI Aayog to nurture, handhold and support New Age Entrepreneurs for New Age India. The focus is on job creation through entrepreneurship in the domains of Agriculture, Education & ICT.


It also aims to create & support sustainable business models at grass root level including social enterprises. AIC-RMP also motivates youngsters and entrepreneurs to innovate, to build and run successful working (business) models and to use information communication technology to support and strengthen self help groups, cooperatives, agriculture sector, education sector and social sector.

Website: https://www.aicbimtech.com/

Email ID: contact@aic-rmp.org
