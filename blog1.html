Customer needs have evolved and so has Marketing in this era. Marketing is more localized with a tinge of globalization. Customers today want to be unique but at the same time want to compete on global platforms. This is no easy task to cater to the marketing needs of such specifications.

Marketing is not simply creating high-resolution images, or kick-ass videos, or digital campaigns; Marketing is a science wherein you have to understand the consumers’ psychology and act accordingly. In today’s era, the time taken for retention in consumers’ minds is hardly in seconds, and you have to effectively use that span of seconds to acquire your brand space in their minds. Consumers move to the next content with a blink of an eye. Content should match the viewers’ psychology, then only they will have the brand recall in their mind in the future.

Modern marketing is all about understanding consumers' mindset, preferences, and needs in-depth to impose brand loyalty. Hence, marketing stakeholders are sharpening their axes to leverage the newest science of this era, to not only retain the customer base but also to acquire new customers from their competitors’ landscape. To compete on global stages marketers are left with no other choice than to adopt these modern marketing practices.
